Our goal is to help change the public narrative around energy from pessimism or deluded optimism to realism. If plans are realistic they enable and encourage long term hope and actions for whatever people want: like energy security on one side or green sustainability on the other.

We want people to have the same base line of facts to enable useful conversations. We will let others debate if a particular approaches like 'green house gas neutral' are desirable and necessary.
